## Avatars — DiceBear

LokiDoki displays avatars generated by [DiceBear](https://www.dicebear.com).

- **DiceBear core library** ([`@dicebear/core`](https://github.com/dicebear/dicebear)) — MIT
- **DiceBear collections** ([`@dicebear/collection`](https://github.com/dicebear/dicebear)) — individual avatar styles are licensed under various terms, many of which are **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY-4.0)**.

### CC-BY-4.0 Attribution

As required by CC-BY-4.0, we credit the authors of the avatar styles included with DiceBear. Style-level attribution is shown in the LokiDoki UI under **Settings → About → Avatar credits**. Style authors and their license terms are documented by DiceBear at <https://www.dicebear.com/licenses/>.

> **If you fork LokiDoki or redistribute it with DiceBear avatars included, you must preserve this attribution.**
